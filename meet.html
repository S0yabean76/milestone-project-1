Who Are We?

We are 3 mum's from Sheffield, who dabble in the art of making music in our spare time! We're by no means professional, and usually stick to the safety of our own living rooms!

Why did we decide to perform?

We'd been strumming and singing away quite happily to ourselves for a few months. Then we saw the advert for StandUp2Cancer, and had the bright idea that we could put on a gig to raise money. Couldn't we?! Yes, we could! (I can't lie - wine was involved!)

How Did We Do It?

We practiced - lots! And asked a local pub if we could 'borrow' a room - which they happily provided as it was for charideee! Then we invited loads of friends and family ('cause we knew they'd be nice to us!) and after much nervousness...we DID IT!
